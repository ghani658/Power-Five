Background

I am a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER ™ professional and Financial Advisor with Wells Fargo Advisors Pine Bluff Arkansas office. With more than 39 years ’ experience as a Financial Advisor, I can help you select investments that fit well with your portfolio objectives. Whether you are an experienced investor or just beginning to invest, you can depend on me for objective advice that focuses on your unique goals. The core focus of my practice involves comprehensive wealth management and planning. This means I work with individuals and families who align to my philosophy of investing and value my advice. I have been passionate about working with my clients since 1982.

As a financial advisor, I help manage assets for individuals, trusts, retirement plans, charitable organizations and businesses. Through a long-term investment approach, I help clients develop and implement strategies for setting goals and building wealth.

The mission of my team is to build lifelong client relationships built on the foundation of trust and client satisfaction. Our value is built on our experience and knowledge of investment planning, investments and financial markets. We work with our clients to formulate a comprehensive plan, implement that plan to provide ongoing guidance and direction. At no time do we forget whose money and whose future is in our hands. Your trust and confidence is our guide.

I obtained a bachelor ’ s degree in marketing from the University of Arkansas, Fayetteville. In 1982 I began my career with Dean Witter Reynolds. In 1987 I joined AG Edwards and Sons a predecessor firm of Wells Fargo Advisors as a financial advisor. In 2002 I earned the certification of CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER ™

Since 1982, I have been married to my wife, Terri Jones Cash, of Pine Bluff, Arkansas. We have two children. Our son and daughter-in-law Chris and Amanda Cash live in Mountain Home Arkansas with our grandchildren, Taylor and Hudson. Our daughter and son-in-law, Katie and Payton Smith live in North Little Rock, Arkansas with our grandchildren Harrison and Sarah Kate.

I am currently the Administrative Board Chairman of First United Methodist Church in Pine Bluff Arkansas, on the board of CASA Inc. in Pine Bluff Arkansas, the Investment Committee at Jefferson Regional Medical Center.

Making sure your wealth continues to work in support of the goals you have established takes careful planning. Such planning is not a single event – it is a process. As your life circumstances change, so must the investment strategies I use to stay on course toward meeting your objectives. That is why I work with you, one-on-one, to design investment strategies targeted to your specific needs and goals. Although I do not provide tax advice, I will help you stay informed with tax issues and estate plan issues to help preserve and protect your wealth, now and for generations to come.
