Robert Renicker

CFP ®

Robert Renicker

Background & Experience

Rob began his financial services career working alongside his father, Bob Renicker as an Investment Associate with a competing firm. In 2006, they moved their practice to Wachovia Securities (now Wells Fargo Advisors). In 2015, Bob passed away. Rob is honored to have been able to work with his dad for 11.5 years and he considers it a privilege to carry on the team that they built together.

Rob earned an Associate in Arts degree in General Studies at Tyler Junior College and Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration at Louisiana State University in Shreveport. In addition to holding the Series 7 & 66 securities registrations & insurance license, he also earned the CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ designation. In 2019, 2020 & 2021 Rob was named a Premier Advisor with Wells Fargo Advisors. The premier advisor distinction is held by a select group of Financial Advisors as measured by completion of educational components, business production based on either of the past two years, and professionalism. Additional criteria, including length of service, may also be used to determine recipients.

Focus

Rob believes that each client is unique and he enjoys getting to know each of them so he can better guide them through each phase of the investment process. He is a good listener and communicator. Rob strives to understand his client’s goals and dreams in addition to their objectives & risk tolerance before creating and implementing an individualized investment plan.

Away From The Office

Rob and his wife, Meghan live in Tyler with their twins, Robby & Jocelyn. They enjoy attending their children’s activities and spending time together as a family. One of their favorite pastimes is watching football and Rob enjoys playing golf. Rob is active in his Christian faith through worship at Green Acres Baptist Church. He currently serves as an Insurance Committee Member. He is a member of The Tyler New Comers Club and is a member of the Louisiana Tech, LSU Shreveport and Tau Kappa Epsilon Fraternity Alumni Associations.
